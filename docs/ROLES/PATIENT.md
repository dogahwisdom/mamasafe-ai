# Patient Portal Guide

Complete guide for patients using MamaSafe AI.

## Overview

The Patient Portal is designed to help expecting mothers track their pregnancy, manage medications, and access healthcare resources.

## Key Features

- **Dashboard** - Overview of pregnancy status
- **Symptom Reporting** - AI-powered triage system
- **Medication Tracking** - Daily medication management
- **Education** - Pregnancy resources and information
- **Appointments** - Schedule and track clinic visits

## Getting Started

### First Login

1. Navigate to the login page
2. Enter your credentials
3. You'll be taken to your dashboard
4. Review your pregnancy information

### Dashboard Tour

Your dashboard shows:
- Current gestation week
- Risk status
- Next appointment date
- Daily medication schedule
- Quick action buttons

## Daily Usage

### Morning Routine

1. Check dashboard for today's medications
2. Review any notifications
3. Mark medications as taken when you take them

### Reporting Symptoms

1. Click "Report Symptoms"
2. Describe your symptoms clearly
3. Submit for analysis
4. Review results and recommendations
5. Follow recommended actions

### Medication Management

1. View daily schedule on dashboard
2. Mark medications as taken
3. Check medication details if needed
4. Track adherence over time

## Best Practices

- Report symptoms promptly
- Take medications as prescribed
- Keep appointments
- Review educational content regularly
- Update profile information when needed
